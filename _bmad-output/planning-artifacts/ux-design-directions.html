Evaluation Guide

QUICK-ADD CONTEXT

Which direction lets you reach the add form fastest? Directions 2, 7, and 8 prioritize immediate capture. Directions 1, 3, 4, 5, 6 require one extra tap via FAB.

MONTHLY REVIEW CONTEXT

Directions 6 makes the drill-down most obvious. Directions 1, 3, 4 emphasize the total + breakdown. Direction 5 emphasizes the raw list.

LIGHT / DARK

Directions 3 and 8 show the dark theme. All others are light. The final design supports both — pick the light-mode direction you prefer.

MIX AND MATCH

You can combine elements. E.g. Dir 6's category list + Dir 7's add sheet is a common and strong combination.
